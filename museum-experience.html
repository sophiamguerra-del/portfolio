Museum as a Shared Experience

Read the full paper

Download project PDF ↓ Open in new tab →

Context

A written project exploring how museum visits can be transformed from solitary viewing into shared, social experiences through design and facilitation.

My role

Researcher and author. Explored experience design frameworks and applied them to a cultural institution context.

What I explored

The role of space, programming, and interaction design in enabling connection between visitors — and the design levers museums can use to facilitate shared meaning-making.

Outcome

Academic project; findings relevant to experience design, cultural institutions, and social UX.
